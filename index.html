PolyViral

A content creation platform for creators who want to turn public prediction market data into engaging short-form videos and publish them to their own TikTok accounts.

For Creators · Finance · Automated Video

What is PolyViral?

PolyViral is a web-based tool that helps creators produce short-form vertical videos about prediction market outcomes sourced from public Polymarket data. Creators sign in to PolyViral, connect their own TikTok account via TikTok Login Kit, generate a video from a selection of recently resolved markets, and publish the finished MP4 directly to their own TikTok account through the TikTok Content Posting API.

PolyViral does not operate a single publisher account. Every video published through PolyViral is posted to the authenticated creator's own TikTok account, under their own control, with their own caption and privacy settings.

How PolyViral works

  1. Sign in with TikTok — The creator authenticates their own TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit and grants PolyViral permission to publish videos on their behalf.
  2. Select markets — The creator browses recently resolved Polymarket prediction markets and picks the outcomes they want to feature in the video.
  3. Generate the video — PolyViral renders a 1080x1920 MP4 (60-70 seconds) using the Remotion rendering engine, with the market questions, outcomes, and public trade data the creator selected.
  4. Review and publish — The creator previews the video, writes a caption, chooses the privacy level (Public / Friends / Only Me), and PolyViral uploads the MP4 to the creator's own TikTok account through the Content Posting API.

TikTok API integration

Login Kit

Used to authenticate each creator's own TikTok account so they can publish videos through PolyViral. Scope requested: user.info.basic. PolyViral only stores the minimum profile data needed to display the connected account to the creator.

Content Posting API

Used to upload and publish the creator-reviewed MP4 to the creator's own authenticated TikTok account. Scopes requested: video.publish, video.upload. Creators explicitly trigger each publication from the PolyViral interface.

Creator control

Creators choose the caption, hashtags, and privacy setting for every video before publication. They can disconnect their TikTok account from PolyViral at any time, which revokes all access tokens and removes stored profile data.

Compliance with TikTok policies

  • PolyViral complies with the TikTok Developer Terms of Service, Developer Guidelines, and Content Sharing Guidelines.
  • PolyViral does not remove, alter, or obscure any TikTok watermarks, branding, or copyright notices.
  • PolyViral only publishes videos that the authenticated creator has reviewed and explicitly chosen to publish to their own account.
  • PolyViral does not spam, automate account actions beyond creator-initiated video publication, or interact with other TikTok users' content.
  • Access tokens are stored encrypted and are used only for the scopes the creator granted.

Data sources

  • Polymarket public API — publicly available resolved market questions, outcomes, and public pseudonymous trade data already visible on polymarket.com.
  • TikTok Login Kit — basic public profile information of the creator who signed in, used only to display their connected account inside PolyViral.
  • PolyViral does not collect or process any personal data from TikTok viewers.
