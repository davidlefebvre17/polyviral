PolyViral - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

PolyViral respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (almost none) and how we use it. PolyViral is an automated video publishing tool — we do not operate a user-facing application, website with accounts, or any service that collects personal data from individuals.

2. Personal Data Collection

PolyViral does not collect any personal data from users. Specifically:

  • We do not have user accounts or login systems
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not collect or store any TikTok user data
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes

3. Public Market Data

We access publicly available data from the Polymarket API, including:

  • Public market questions and resolution outcomes
  • Public trade data (pseudonymous usernames, trade amounts, prices)
  • Public leaderboard rankings

All this data is already publicly visible on polymarket.com to anyone. We do not access any private or authenticated Polymarket data.

4. TikTok API Usage

We use the TikTok Content Posting API exclusively to publish original video content to our own TikTok account (@polyviral). Our use of the TikTok API is limited to:

  • Scope: video.publish — posting videos to our own account
  • We do not access, read, or store any TikTok user data, profiles, or content
  • We do not use TikTok data for any purpose other than publishing our own videos
  • We comply fully with TikTok's Developer Guidelines, Content Sharing Guidelines, and all applicable policies
  • We do not remove, alter, or obscure any TikTok watermarks or branding

5. Third-Party Services

Our pipeline uses the following third-party services for content generation (not for user data processing):

  • Polymarket API — public prediction market data
  • TikTok Content Posting API — video publishing to our own account
  • Anthropic Claude API — text generation for content scripts
  • Fish Audio API — voice-over generation

None of these services receive any personal user data from us.

6. Data Storage

Generated videos, narration audio, and market data snapshots are stored locally on our own infrastructure for content management and deduplication purposes. No personal data is stored.

7. Children's Privacy

PolyViral content involves financial predictions and is intended for audiences 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you believe your public Polymarket trading data should not be featured in our content, please contact us and we will address your concern.

9. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

10. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact us at polyviral@proton.me
